# Tempus Product Knowledge Base
## Overview
Tempus leverages one of the world’s largest libraries of clinical and molecular data to provide physicians with actionable insights. This knowledge base serves as the "Source of Truth" for the Sales Copilot to bridge the data-to-insight gap.

---

## 1. Tempus xT (Broad Panel Solid Tumor)
* **Description:** A comprehensive targeted assay designed to detect actionable genomic alterations in solid tumors.
* **Gene Coverage:** 648 DNA genes.
* **Methodology:** DNA sequencing + Whole Transcriptome Sequencing (RNA).
* **Supported Cancers:** All malignant solid tumors (e.g., Lung, Breast, Colorectal, Pancreatic).
* **Turnaround Time (TAT):** 9–14 days.
* **Accuracy Metric:** 28% reduction in false-positive calls through Tumor + Normal matching.
* **Clinical Utility:** Identifies SNVs, indels, CNVs, and rearrangements. Includes MSI and TMB status.

## 2. Tempus xR (Whole Transcriptome)
* **Description:** Unbiased RNA sequencing that complements DNA testing to provide a complete molecular picture.
* **Gene Coverage:** 20,000+ genes (Whole Transcriptome).
* **Methodology:** Whole Transcriptome Sequencing (RNA).
* **Supported Cancers:** Integrated with all solid tumor and hematologic malignancy testing.
* **Turnaround Time (TAT):** Delivered concurrently with xT (9–14 days).
* **Accuracy Metric:** Identifies **29% to 33% more actionable fusions** often missed by DNA-only panels.
* **Clinical Utility:** Superior detection of gene fusions (NTRK, ALK, ROS1) and splice variants (MET exon 14).

## 3. Tempus xF / xF+ (Liquid Biopsy)
* **Description:** Non-invasive blood-based test for patients when tissue is unavailable or for monitoring resistance.
* **Gene Coverage:** 105 genes (ctDNA).
* **Methodology:** Circulating tumor DNA (ctDNA) sequencing.
* **Supported Cancers:** Advanced solid tumors; frequently used in Lung, Breast, and Ovarian cancers.
* **Turnaround Time (TAT):** 7–10 days (xF+ may take up to 9–10 days).
* **Accuracy Metric:** >99.9% specificity at ≥0.25% Variant Allele Frequency (VAF).
* **Clinical Utility:** Ideal for "tissue-first" failures or rapid progression scenarios.

## 4. Tempus xH (Heme - Myeloid & Lymphoid)
* **Description:** Targeted panel specifically for hematologic malignancies.
* **Gene Coverage:** 600+ DNA genes + Whole Transcriptome (RNA).
* **Methodology:** DNA + RNA Sequencing.
* **Supported Cancers:** Leukemia (AML), Lymphoma, Multiple Myeloma, and MDS.
* **Turnaround Time (TAT):** ~9 days (Expedited for acute clinical needs).
* **Accuracy Metric:** Combines DNA/RNA to capture prognostic markers missed by single-modality tests.

## 5. Tempus xG / xG+ (Hereditary Cancer)
* **Description:** Germline test to identify inherited genetic mutations that increase cancer risk.
* **Gene Coverage:** 88 genes associated with hereditary cancer syndromes.
* **Methodology:** Germline DNA sequencing (Blood or Saliva).
* **Supported Cancers:** Risk assessment for Breast, Ovarian, Colorectal, and Pancreatic cancers.
* **Turnaround Time (TAT):** 14–21 days (Expedited 7–10 day TAT available for standalone BRCAplus).
* **Clinical Utility:** Informs surgical decisions and risk assessment for family members.

## 6. Tempus xO (Whole Exome)
* **Description:** Ultra-comprehensive research-grade panel for deep genomic exploration.
* **Gene Coverage:** ~19,000+ genes (Protein-coding regions).
* **Methodology:** Whole Exome Sequencing (WES).
* **Supported Cancers:** Complex or rare cases where standard panels are inconclusive.
* **Turnaround Time (TAT):** 8–10 days for sequencing completion.
* **Clinical Utility:** Primary use in clinical trial matching and deep research.

## 7. Tempus xM (Minimal Residual Disease - MRD)
* **Description:** Personalized, tumor-informed liquid biopsy assay to detect molecular residual disease.
* **Methodology:** Custom-designed based on the patient's unique tumor signature from initial xT test.
* **Supported Cancers:** Serial monitoring for recurrence in previously diagnosed patients.
* **Turnaround Time (TAT):** Varies based on serial draw schedule.
* **Clinical Utility:** Monitors for recurrence earlier than standard imaging.

## 8. AI-Enabled Tests (HRD & TO)
* **HRD (Homologous Recombination Deficiency):** Digital pathology/genomic hybrid; predicts response to PARP inhibitors.
* **TO (Tumor-Only) Algorithmic Normalization:** Machine learning that differentiates germline from somatic mutations when a normal sample is unavailable.
* **Accuracy Metric:** 99.9% specificity in variant calling for Tumor-Only scenarios.
